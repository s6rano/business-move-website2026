# What budget for an office move by size?

**The first thing that determines an office move budget is the scale of the project. A small office, an SME floor or a headquarters do not have the same volume, the same complexity or the same price. Placing your company in the right order of magnitude already frames your budget.**

Here are benchmarks by size, to be taken for what they are: orders of magnitude, not a price list. They then adjust to access, IT and timing.

> The budget is read first by size, then refined to your situation. These benchmarks give a rough guide; they do not replace a quote.

## Two ways to cost it, first of all

There are two ways to approach a budget, and they overlap.

The first works per workstation: often count €200 to €300 per workstation, sometimes only €100 to €200 for light equipment, clearly more if you have servers or specific hardware. The second works by volume: in the region of €25 to €60 per cubic metre, counting on average three cubic metres of office furniture per workstation. These two approaches give a first frame; the rest depends on your situation.

## The small office (a few workstations)

A small team, a few workstations, often a single floor: the volume is modest and the operation usually fits in a day. Often count €1,500 to €5,000.

At this scale, one factor weighs proportionally more: access. A floor without a lift or difficult parking can make up a notable share of the bill. IT, however limited, also counts.

## The floor or the SME (twenty to fifty workstations)

An open space or several offices, more substantial IT, archives to handle: complexity rises a notch. Often count €5,000 to €20,000. As a guide, for around twenty people you see quotes in the region of €190 to €350 per person, depending on the city and complexity.

At this stage, internal coordination becomes a genuine issue: someone has to steer the project, compare offers against a precise brief and secure the restart.

## The headquarters or large organisation (above that)

Several floors or sites, a large IT estate, critical business continuity, sometimes a phased move: here logistics weigh as much as transport. Count €20,000 to more than €100,000.

Out-of-hours or weekend work, data security and phasing to avoid interrupting the business become items in their own right.

## The "per workstation" benchmark, and its limit

Working "per workstation" gives a quick first approach, whatever the size. But this logic has a limit: it tends to favour large structures. For an SME, access and IT can push the real cost per workstation well above the average benchmark.

## What moves the budget in your category

Whatever your size, four factors shift the bill of a corporate move: access (floors, furniture hoist), IT, timing (evening, weekend) and distance. As a precaution, keep a reserve of about 10% for the unexpected. We detail these factors and how a quote is built in dedicated articles.

## Estimate your case precisely

Benchmarks by size give an indication; they do not replace an estimate. To approach your actual budget:

1. make a clear inventory of what is moving, going into storage or being discarded;
2. use our calculator for a first indicative estimate;
3. request three comparable quotes, after a site visit.

## In summary

An office move budget is read first by size: small office, floor or SME, headquarters. Then it is refined to access, IT and timing. Use these benchmarks to get your bearings, the calculator for a first estimate, and three comparable quotes to decide.
