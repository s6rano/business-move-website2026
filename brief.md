# Project Brief: Business Move Landing Page

## Project Overview
**Business Move** is a premium corporate relocation service provider based in Belgium. The project aims to deliver a high-fidelity, trilingually accessible (FR, EN, NL) landing page that emphasizes precision, logistics intelligence, and professional reliability. The design follows a "clean and professional" aesthetic with a warm, neutral color palette and modern typography.

## Target Audience
- Large enterprises and SMEs in Belgium.
- Facility managers and HQ relocation coordinators.
- International companies moving to or within Belgium.

## Brand Identity
- **Brand Name:** Business Move
- **Tone & Voice:** Professional, Precise, Premium, Trustworthy.
- **Design Aesthetic:** Minimalist, high contrast, clean hierarchy, and smooth motion.

## Technical Specifications
- **Framework:** Vanilla HTML5, CSS3 (Tailwind CSS), and JavaScript.
- **Interactivity:** Scroll-triggered animations, hover effects, and smooth transitions.
- **Localization:** Trilingual support (French, English, Dutch).
- **Modes:** Support for Light Mode (Primary) and Dark Mode.
- **Platform Strategy:** Design validated in Stitch, deployed via Codex using a Markdown-first content approach.

## Visual Language (Design System)
- **Primary Font:** Montserrat (Web-safe alternative to Myriad Pro).
- **Core Palette:**
  - Background: `#bfb4b0` (Warm Neutral)
  - Primary Text/UI: `#333333` & `#3c3c3b`
  - Accents: High-contrast dark containers for emphasis.
- **Shape Language:** Subtle roundness (4px) to maintain a corporate yet approachable feel.

## Key Sections & Features
1.  **Global Navigation:** Sticky header with language switcher (FR/NL/EN) and primary CTA ("Get Quote").
2.  **Hero Section:** High-impact value proposition ("Votre partenaire de mobilité d'entreprise en Belgique") with a clear "Request Quote" CTA.
3.  **Solutions Grid:** Three-pillar focus on Office Relocation, IT Logistics, and Furniture Storage.
4.  **Eco-Friendly Commitment:** Dedicated section for carbon-neutral logistics and sustainability reporting.
5.  **Interactive Quote Calculator:** Lead generation form allowing users to select relocation types and origin cities.
6.  **Trust Signals:** Real-time stats (99.8% Punctuality Rate) and liability coverage badges.
7.  **Multi-Column Footer:** Corporate links, regional office info, and contact details.

## Success Metrics
- Seamless transition from visual design to Codex deployment.
- High engagement with the "Calculez mon devis" calculator.
- Professional brand perception through consistent trilingual content and motion design.
